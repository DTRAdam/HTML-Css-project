About Me

Hello, my name is Adam.

I am 22 years old. One thing about me is that I always had an interest in programming since the day I met a really good programmer in high school. This even made me consider studying with him because he was my roommate.

The thing that attracted me to programming even more is the fact that I can use this knowledge for myself and my day to day use on computers.


But why specifically website building?

Well, for this question, I would answer with the most simple answer which is I love to use my computer a lot so why not learn something that I can do and challenge myself with it and create a website that can visually look appealing and useful for me or for others.

So as you can see this is why I chose to learn website building.
